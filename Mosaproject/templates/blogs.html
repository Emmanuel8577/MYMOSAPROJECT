BUT BE YE TRANSFORMED BY THE RENEWING OF YOUR MIND

The mind is often understood as a faculty that manifests itself in mental phenomena like sensation, perception, thinking, reasoning, memory,belief, desire, emotion, and Motivation. Minds are defined as independent thinking substances.
In popular usage, mind is frequently synonymous with thought: the private conversation with ourselves that we carry on “inside our head”. Thus, we “make up our minds”, or “change our minds”.

One of the key attributes of the mind in this sense is that, it is a private sphere to which no one but the owner has access. No one else can “know our mind”. They can only interpret what we consciously or unconsciously communicate. Prov. 23:7 says, "For as a Man thinketh in his heart, so is he”. What a man consciously thinks in his heart he gradually becomes, what a man gives his attention to he gradually becomes.
Beloved, whatever you give your attention to, you gradually become it. If you think little of yourself, you will gradually become little.
There is a saying that" you can't stop a bird from flying over your head, but you can definitely stop it from perching over your head. Thoughts can easily be formed especially when the mind is idle and not engaged, and the devil wouldn't hesitate to sponsor any negative thoughts when the mind is idle Scriptures says, "But be ye Transformed by the Renewing of the mind” Transformed is a Greek word called “metarmorphoo” which means change, while Renewing means to Renovate, Revamp or Replenish.
What that means in a simple term is that, you change your thoughts by replacing good thoughts over the evil ones.
Don't let the devil cage you in your thoughts that you're small or cannot make it in life The devil has caged many through thoughts in their lustfulness for modern things, many spirit filled brothers' & Sisters' can no longer pray effectively because they have been caged in their thoughts over the cares of this life.
Most spirit filled brethren have been hooked up in the online space with one thing or the other, others visit porn sites secretly with their phones and quickly deletes history. But beloved, if you must think right, you must renew your thoughts with knowledge. (Col. 3:10)
Seek knowledge, read good books, listen to good songs, messages and pray in the Holy Ghost to keep your mind renewed.
Your mind is your greatest asset for greater possibilities in God. God has invested so much possibilities inside of you that you cannot imagine.
Finally, beloved, whatsoever things are true, whatsoever things are honest, whatsoever things are just, whatsoever things are pure, whatsoever things are lovely, whatsoever things are of good report; if there be any virtue, and if there be any praise, THINK on these.(Phil.4:8, italics added) Remember, only give yourself to positive thoughts and change any evil thought that doesn't edify your soul. Until I come your way again, do have a great week ahead! Greater exploit I pray

Emmanuel Adikwu
