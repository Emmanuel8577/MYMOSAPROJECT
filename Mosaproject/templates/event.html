About Mosa

Mosa is an acronym which stands for Mkar Old Student Association,
It is a body that comprised of all former students who attended NKST COLLEGE MKAR, GBOKO, BENUE STATE
who desiring to come together in unity have wilfully established this distinguished Association
to cater for the needs of her Members as well as the interests of
our great Alma mater.
